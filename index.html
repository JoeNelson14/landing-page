JDM Iconic Cars

The Mazda-Rx7 is one of the most iconic cars. Its rotary engine makes it special as most cars did not have this style engine. It has an amazing body style, iconic exhuast sound, 10,000 RPM range, and in some body styles has pop up headlights.

The GTR Skyline R34 is wanted by many people today. Its 2.6L twin tubro charged engine RB26 is able to make crazy hp from factory with supporting upgrades. Many people will say this is one of the best engines ever made. It doesn't end there tho, it also has an amazing body style that turns peoples heads.

The Toyota Supra MKIV is another iconic car that become really popular from the Fast & Furious movie. The movie isn't the only thing people know this car for, it has another most saught after enigne in it, the 2jz. The 2jz allows for good hp from factory and with some upgrades it can reach high hp goals that people want.

THe Mitsubishi Evo 6, 8, and 9 is another car that can be found in the Fast & Furious movie. With it's AWD it handles corner like they are nothing. With its 4G63 inline 4 engine you can put some upgrades on it and reach good hp levels. This car looks good, sounds good, and tracks great.
